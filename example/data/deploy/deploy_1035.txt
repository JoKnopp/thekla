The new Afghanistan war strategy that President Barack Obama delivered last night at West Point , which consists of deploying an additional 30000 US troops into the region within the next 6 months , was met with responses that varied dramatically worldwide. Some of the reactions that were broadly reported included glorious praise from UN and NATO allies , skepticism from Afghan citizens , lukewarm consent from some neoconservatives , and vitriol from the most unfathomable of bedfellows - Dick Cheney , the Taliban , and far left-wing liberals. www.examiner.com
