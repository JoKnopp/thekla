Acton , MA ( PRWEB ) January 27 , 2010 -- Global Graphics ( NYSE-Euronext : GLOG ) : Around half of large organizations ( 51 percent ) will deploy more enterprise-wide free software in 2010 , according to new research. The trend is partly driven by the pressure on enterprise IT budgets; with two thirds of CIOs saying their budget is the same or less than in 2009. The full report is available at globalgraphics.com/freesoftware .
