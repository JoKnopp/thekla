7th TTSB cases colors for deployment MANNHEIM , Germany ( April 27 , 2009 ) The command team of Col. Randall Bland ( left ) and Command Sgt. Maj. Terence Farmer ( right ) from 5th Signal Command 's 7th Theater Tactical Signal Brigade headquartered in Mannheim , Germany cased their colors in a ceremony April 27 at the Sports Arena on Sullivan Barracks. The brigade 's headquarters element will deploy this spring for up to 12 months with approximately 90 Soldiers to Afghanistan in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. The brigade headquarters will serve as the Joint Network Operations Control Center which is the single control agency for the management of the joint force communications systems in Afghanistan.
