Since 2003 , when the first Stryker unit deployed to Iraq , the vehicles have been painted deep green. When the 5th Stryker Brigade , Fort Lewis , Wash. , deployed to Afghanistan several months ago , the first Stryker BCT to do so , the vehicles were standard green , and they will continue to be produced in that color. The Army recently announced , however , that as the vehicles are sent to authorized facilities for repair and retrofit , they will be repainted desert tan to fit in better with the surrounding terrain .
