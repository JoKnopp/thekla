A Windows free SIP IP PBX software. It is very easy to use and deploy , with powerful features like : ACD , Auto Attendant , Call Park , Ring Group , Conference , Call Pickup Group , Auto Outbound Dialer , Database CDR report , Database PBX Status Report. It supports SIP extensions , SIP accounts , inbound and outbound calls , and customized dial plans. It supports plugins. Plugin samples can be programmed by .NET languange like C , and vb.net .
