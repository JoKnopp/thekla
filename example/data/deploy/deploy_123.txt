RA'ANANA , Israel; October 15 , 2008 - Retalix ( r ) Ltd. ( NASDAQ : RTLX ) , a leading provider of software solutions for retailers and distributors , announced today that Morrisons ( LSE : MRW ) , the UK 's fourth largest supermarket group , will deploy Retalix software to increase operational efficiency and enhance the shopping experience across its 378 stores and 285 filling stations .
