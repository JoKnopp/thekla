A recent survey by Gartner points to the growing acceptance of Software as a Service as a cost-effective and fast way to deploy new software with 90 of respondents affirming the use of SaaS. Respondents to the survey also cite replacement of on-premses solutions and net-new implementations as major drivers of future deployments. The key drivers cited included total cost of ownership ( TCO ) , and unmet performance expectations with on-premises solutions , in addition to changes in sourcing strategy .
