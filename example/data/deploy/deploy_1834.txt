Singapore ( Agencies ) : Singapore ' s Deputy Prime Minister and Defense Minister Teo Chee Hean said that the country is prepared to deploy a weapon locating radar team to central Afghanistan , local media reported on Wednesday. According to local radio 938live , Teo said the WLR team will be deployed to Tarin Kowt in Oruzgan province in Afghanistan , and it will provide early warning of rocket attacks and enhance force protection measures of the ISAF personnel deployed in the base there .
