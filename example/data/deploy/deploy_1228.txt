Crushing EXO-FORCE defenses , here comes the unstoppable Mobile Devastator ! Commanded by robot leader Meca One , the ultimate wheeled fortress opens up to reveal an elevating battle tower with rotating laser cannons and energy sphere launchers , twin scout fliers , prison pods with skeletons , and a legion of robot warriors equipped and ready to deploy into battle. The only thing that stands in its way is Ryo in the Blazing Falcon battle machine with slashing claws and firing rocket !
