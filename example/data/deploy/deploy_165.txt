The training being received by the federal employees is part of a three-week block of courses that all servicemembers receive prior to deployment , which includes classes such as combat lifesaving , cultural awareness , weapons and equipment familiarization , and tactics training , to name a few. The training culminates into an eight-day exercise , designed to test the PRT 's by utilizing Atterbury 's immersive , realistic training environment , which includes multiple training scenarios that each team must collectively accomplish before deploying .
