[ 0105 ] The first part of service state 518 lists the service functions selected and enabled. For each one , service state 518 describes the observed and expected performance ranges. The second part of service state 518 lists the actual service instances selected to support this service. Service state 518 also describes current usage of each supporting service instance by this service , service level agreements with each service instance , and any violations. It also describes various attributes of the current state e.g. , it may state that the service is deployed in dedicated mode or in shared mode and may point to corresponding policies or business rules. Similarly , the third part of service state 518 lists the actual service instances supported by this service instance , demand placed by those service instances , and current usage by the supported services. Service state 518 also lists any service level agreements with the supported services and any violations .
