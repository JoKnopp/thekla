This circular invites HE institutions in England to submit funding proposals for projects to build and deploy Virtual Research Environments ( VREs ) , based on currently available tools and frameworks. Funding of £ 3.2 million is available over two years from the date of award. As a general guideline it is anticipated that projects will be awarded funding of between £ 50 , 000 and £ 300 , 000 depending on the extend of the work involved .
