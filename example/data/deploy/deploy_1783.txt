Hybrid License. A Hybrid License allows a customer to deploy its Application Software for internal use to an unlimited number of computers that are owned or controlled by the customer. In addition , a customer may commercially deploy up to one thousand ( 1000 ) client licenses or five ( 5 ) production servers of its Application Software per year with this license. If a customer wishes to deploy more than one thousand ( 1000 ) copies/five ( 5 ) production servers of its Application Software per year , the customer must purchase a distribution license. Distribution licenses for single and multi-year periods may be purchased for a reasonable flat fee. Please contact oemsales leadtools.com to inquire about a distribution license .
