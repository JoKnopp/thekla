One of the biggest surprises for ISVs is the overhead involved in supporting their software round the clock on a 24/7 basis. They 're used to helping customers deploy their software , but providing ongoing service is a very different concept. Software companies are n't conscious of the extent of the impact that downtime has on the customer , for instance during upgrades or configuration changes. But in an ASP environment , customers expect ASP services to be available 24 hours a day when they are n't , it causes problems and ill will. The end user does n't care that an upgrade needs to be made all they care about is that they ca n't access their data for a period of time .
