He almost did not deploy overseas. Due to the nature of Soler 's contract with the Navy , he would have been unable to deploy overseas after the first of January 2010. Soler was assigned to the 3rd Battalion 6th Marine Regiment. As it became evident that 3/6 would not deploy until after the first of the year , Mike Soler voluntarily applied for a transfer , and was denied twice before being assigned to 1st Battalion 6th Marine Regiment , which deployed in early December as the first troops in part of President Obama 's Surge .
