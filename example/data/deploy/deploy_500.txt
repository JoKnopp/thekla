System 100 can include a deployment control system 102 that can be utilized by an administrator to deploy software components to one or more target computing devices 110 , 112 and infrastructure resources. The administrator can utilize the deployment control system 102 to install , update , and maintain software components in the target computing devices 110 , 112 of the target computer infrastructure 106 , 108 .
