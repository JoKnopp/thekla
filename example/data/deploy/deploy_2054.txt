Hohenfels , Germany -- Slovenian soldiers carry mock causality during a simulated-combat exercise at the Joint Multinational Readiness Center here. During the exercise , Slovenian soldiers played the role of Afghan soldiers to help prepare Michigan National guardsmen and the Latvian Army , who are part of an Observer Mentor Liaison Team , to deploy to Afghanistan to coach , teach and mentor an Afghan National Army unit.
