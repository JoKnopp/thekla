Pocket PC is a popular platform for mobile devices that supports a wide range of multimedia features. Flash Player 7 for Pocket PC enables developers and publishers to quickly and easily deploy rich interactive Flash content and applications for Windows Mobile 5 and Pocket PC 2003 devices. Developers can view Flash content within Pocket IE or full-screen using third-party software , or deploy stand-alone Flash applications , which requires the Distribution Kit.
