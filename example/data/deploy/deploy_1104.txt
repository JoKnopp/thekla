DENVER , CO -- ( Marketwire ) -- 01/19/09 -- After several months of research and evaluation , Whittier California-based STC Management has decided to deploy Enterprise Software Systems ' CRE Sync property management solution. With near 100 occupancy in the more than 50 properties that STC manages for its clients , STC is at the top of its game. However , with an eye on a bad economy and potential tenant churn , STC is planning for the future .
