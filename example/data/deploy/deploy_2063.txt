72nd Expeditionary Signal Battalion says final farewells MANNHEIM , Germany ( July 16 , 2009 ) -- Col. Gerald H. Miller , commander , 2nd Signal Brigade , addresses the Soldiers and Family members of the deploying 72nd Expeditionary Signal Battalion during their farewell ceremony. The ceremony concluded with the deploying Soldiers leaving on busses while their Family members waved goodbye. 72nd is deploying for up to 12 months to Iraq on their third mission is support of Operation Iraqi Freedom.
