ISYS : desktop is designed to quickly and easily connect employees with information that resides on their desktops and networks. Easy to deploy and maintain , ISYS : desktop supports 60 languages and 200+ file formats , including MS Office formats , email , attachments , PDF , HTML , databases and more. ISYS : desktop offers multiple search , navigation and discovery methods , including natural language , Boolean , proximity , phrase matching , fielded , on-the-fly categorization and entity extraction..
