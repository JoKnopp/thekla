Cornell University announced today that the Cornell Center for Advanced Computing ( CAC ) in partnership with Purdue University has received a National Science Foundation ( NSF ) award to deploy The MathWorks MATLAB on the TeraGrid as an experimental computing resource. This initiative will provide seamless parallel MATLAB computational services running on Windows HPC Server 2008 to remote desktop and Science Gateway users with complex analytic and fast simulation requirements. Project supporters include Dell , Microsoft , and The MathWorks .
