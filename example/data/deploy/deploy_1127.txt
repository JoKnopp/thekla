It averages the time required to deploy various types of Army forces for 10 different combinations of origins and destinations. Forces are assumed to come from the West Coast , Gulf Coast , or East Coast of the United States or from Europe. They are assumed to be deployed to various regions , represented by South Korea , the Philippines , Nigeria , Azerbaijan , Djibouti , and Uganda. CBO also considered deployment of light , medium , and heavy forces ( although not all alternatives include all three types of forces ) .
