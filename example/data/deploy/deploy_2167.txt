According to a news release , CNB is now leveraging Postilion ' s broad range of self-service banking solutions to deliver an integrated , consistent customer experience across all of its self-service channels. Using Postilion ' s single platform to deploy Internet banking and voice banking alongside the ATM and card management solutions enables CNB to improve overall customer satisfaction with solutions that are driven by tight data integration.
