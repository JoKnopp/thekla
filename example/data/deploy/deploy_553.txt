The Communist Party of Bangladesh ( CPB ) has expressed its serious concern over the recent government decision to deploy the army countrywide. In a press statement on December 10 expressing their concern , the CPB President , Manzurul Ahsan Khan , and General Secretary Mujahidul Islam Selim said that since the step was taken without any consultation with other government advisors , the real intention of this unilateral decision by the President is seriously questionable .
