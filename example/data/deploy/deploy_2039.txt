FILE - This undated file photo provided by Army Spc. Alexis Hutchinson shows Spc. Hutchinson and her son , Kamani in Savannah , Ga. The Army filed charges Tuesday , Jan. 12 , 2010 against Hutchinson , a single-mom soldier who refused to deploy to Afghanistan last year , arguing she had no family able to care for her infant son. Hutchinson , a 21-year-old Army cook , could face a prison sentence and a dishonorable discharge if she is convicted in a court-martial. ( AP Photo/Alexis Hutchinson ) NO SALES
