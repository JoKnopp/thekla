Second , cloud computing is about flexibility. With cloud-based solutions , you can add or remove Web server hardware and bandwidth , virtually on demand. For example , you can order more computing " horsepower " to match a cyclical business cycle or a big new marketing promotion , but you do n't actually have to purchase or deploy physical servers ... it all happens in the cloud. Also , the software platforms you use in the cloud are often designed to enable nondevelopers to rapidly customize your software 's functionality .
