Deployment-malfunction drill : When faced with an imminent attack ( 1 ) , the practitioner reaches for his knife ( 2 ) . He attempts to deploy the blade , but stress and perspiration cause him to fail ( 3 ) . He then executes his preplanned empty-hand technique or combination ( 4 ) to buy time to attempt to open the blade again ( 5 ) . With the knife now fully deployed , the practitioner can defend himself ( 6 ) .
