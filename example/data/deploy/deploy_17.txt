Sentry locks down your computer on demand so it stays secure when you are n't using it. No other programs can be opened , no browser history snooped at , and nobody will be touching your files while you 're gone. Sentry even takes messages from people who dropped by while you were away. The program appearance is customizable , and can be quickly deployed to a USB flashdrive , floppy disk , or CD to quickly lock any public workstation you may use .
