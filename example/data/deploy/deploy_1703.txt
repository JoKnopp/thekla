Web Services and SOA : As SaaS software gets deployed behind its own firewall , for most enterprise applications , there are strong needs for data integration. Most SaaS products have a rich WS layer that enables them to integrate with third parties. This actually is a blessing in disguise as it provides an easy interface for QA to test the key business functionality through an easy to automate interface.
