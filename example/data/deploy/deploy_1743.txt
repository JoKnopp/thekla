Lockheed Martin will assemble and test the integrated aerostats , sensors , ground stations and mooring systems at its Defense and Surveillance Systems facility in Akron. When deployed , the PTDS will be operated and maintained by Lockheed Martin Technical Operations headquartered in Colorado Springs , CO. The Army 's Program Executive Office for Intelligence , Electronic Warfare and Sensors , Project Manager for Night Vision , Reconnaissance , Surveillance and Target Acquisition , and Product Manager for Robotic and Unmanned Sensors will provide program and acquisition management .
