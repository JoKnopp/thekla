The new sales and marketing information platform will enable Shell Oil Products ( Shell OP ) to continuously fine-tune global customer strategies and seize market opportunities quickly. Shell OP already deploy Kalido Group 's software , KALIDO ( tm ) Dynamic Information Warehouse , to integrate disparate financial data from its semi-autonomous operating units to provide an ongoing , global view of business performance. Based on this success , Shell OP have extended its use of KALIDO Warehouse to gain a better understanding of customer , product and channel profitability both on an operating company and global basis .
