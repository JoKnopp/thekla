The 39-year-old Tunnel Hill , Ga. , resident , eager to serve his country after the terrorist attacks of Sept. 11 , 2001 , deployed to Iraq with the U.S. Army from March 2004 to March 2005. Because he had a college degree , he could have secured a job as an officer away from the front lines. Instead , he enlisted with the rank-and-file infantrymen to make a real sacrifice .
