ActMon Professional is the popular all-round monitoring solution. It allows companies and individuals to track the use / abuse of PCs easily and invisibly. It logs keystrokes , user names , passwords , path names , access times , windows titles , BOTH sides of a CHAT conversation for most popular chat software , AOL content and visited websites. Plus you can send the activity reports invisibly via Email or network , create user specific reports and deploy the software remotely inside your local network .
