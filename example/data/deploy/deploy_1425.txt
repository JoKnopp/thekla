_______ DD FORM 771 EYEWEAR PERSCRIPTION must be completed and returned to the UDC a minimum of three weeks before you deploy , so that your protective mask and ballistic glass inserts can be ordered. This form MUST ALSO be included in your deployment packet , even if glasses/contacts are not required. In that case , simply write " glasses not required " on the form. The DD771 is the only form accepted by DoD when ordering prescription eyewear. NO OTHER FORM OF PRESCRIPTION CAN BE ACCEPTED. ( You can include your civilian prescription with the DD771 . )
