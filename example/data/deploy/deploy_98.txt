7th Theater Tactical Signal Brigade says final farewells MANNHEIM , Germany ( March 31 , 2009 ) -- Brig. Gen. Jeffrey G. Smith , Jr. , commander , 5th Signal Command , says farewell to Spc. Jason B. Haynes , 7th Theater Tactical Signal Brigade , before Haynes and approximately 35 Soldiers from 7th boarded a bus near 7th 's headquarters to deploy to Afghanistan for up to 12 months in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. Family members and friends of the deploying 7th Soldiers gathered to say their farewells to loved ones before the deployment. ( Photo by Staff Sgt. Jonathon M. Gray )
